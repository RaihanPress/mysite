Kia Aap in Dono Buttons ko apne eClass me Add Karna Chahte Hain?

To Please in Steps ko follow karen.


In Buttons ka Kia Kam Hai?

Ye dono button Eclass ke Audio ko aage ya pichhe karne ke liye hain.

Pahla button audio ko das second pichhe kar dega.

Aur dusra button audio ko das second aage kar dega.


In Buttons ko install kaise karen?

Niche diye gaye button par click karen!

Ab Upar Dahini Taraf ↗ 3 dots ⋮ par click karen

Agar Aap Chrome Use karte hain to ☆ Star Button par click karen

Aur Agar Aap Opera Mini Use karte hain to 'Add to Bookmarks' par click karen

Agar Aap Chrome Use karte hain to niche Edit Button par click karen.

Jaha URL ya Address likha hai, uske niche https://faizanahmad.tk/code/ur/ ko hata kar code paste kar den.

Jaha Name likha hai, uske niche koi ek letter likh den, Ab ye kuchh aisa hoga:

Ab Sara kam Ho Chuka!

In Buttons ko Istemal kaise Karen?

Jab bhi Aap eClass me Koi Audio Kholen,

Upar URL par click karen,

Phir Aap ne 'Name' ki Jagah jo letter likha tha, use type karen

Wahi letter sab se niche aa raha hoga, sath hi uske niche ek blue line me javascript: wagairah likha hoga.

Us par click kar den

Aap ka kam ho gaya!
